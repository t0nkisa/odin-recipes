Scrambled eggs

Products

  • 1 Onion
  • 5 Slices of ham
  • 4 Eggs

How to make:

Tip: Get a wooden spoon and move the stuff in the pan so it doesn't get burned

  1. Slice the onion to your liking
  2. Turn up your burner to 5 and add a little bit of oil
  3. Place your sliced onion in the pan and cook until it's sizzling
  4. Slice your ham any way you like and add it in the pan
  5. Cook until the ends start getting brown
  6. Crack the 4 eggs in a bowl, season them with salt, and if you like, red pepper
  7. Beat the eggs until there are no clear egg yolks
  8. Pour the eggs in the pan and start to stir the pan
  9. Push the mix to 1 side, place your spoon in the middle and spin it in a circle
  10. Repeat until you've cooked your eggs to your liking
  11. Serve and enjoy!
